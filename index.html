SP BlockBlast Solver

How to Use

  • Click cells on the Current Board to mark the blocks currently in your game.
  • Use the Available Pieces section to mark the shapes of your three available pieces.
  • Click cells to toggle them on/off. Green cells indicate selected blocks.
  • Use the Clear buttons to reset individual pieces or the main board if you make a mistake.
  • Once you've marked all blocks and pieces, click "Solve Puzzle" to get the solution.
  • The solver will show you the best moves to clear the board using your available pieces.
